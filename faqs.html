Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services do you offer?

We provide haircuts, styling, skincare treatments, makeup services, and more.

2. How can I book an appointment?

You can book online through our website on the Appointments page.

3. Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, but we recommend booking in advance to secure your spot.

4. What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

5. What products do you use?

We use high-quality, salon-grade hair and skincare products.

6. Do you offer bridal or event packages?

Yes! We provide special packages for weddings and events. Contact us for details.

7. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and digital payments.

8. Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Yes, we have a range of products suitable for different skin types.

9. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! You can purchase gift cards for any of our services.

10. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via phone, email, or through the Contact page on our website.
