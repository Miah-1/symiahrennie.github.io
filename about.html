About Cutting Edge

Where passion meets precision in hair and beauty.

Our Story

Cutting Edge began as a small boutique salon powered by a passion for style and dedication. From the very beginning, our mission was clear: to enhance the beauty experience in the Caribbean by merging expert techniques with personalized care. This started as a small, vibrant safe space driven by creativity and community and has evolved into a full-service beauty destination. Now, we’re proud to service a diverse and loyal clientele, where each person is welcomed with the same attitude to helping them look and feel their best.

Our Mission

Our aim is to empower people using cutting-edge products and expert, personal treatment to enhance their natural beauty. We want you to feel and look your best, if that means getting a new haircut, getting a new color treatment, or getting professional skincare advice.
